# Stage 1: Problem, Target Users & Core Value

## The Problem
Women spend hours watching makeup tutorials but have no way to objectively evaluate their own technique. They lack a trusted, private mirror that gives honest but encouraging feedback on their application. Existing alternatives are either:
- YouTube tutorials (generic, not personalized)
- Friends/family (unreliable, subjective)
- Professional makeup artists (expensive, not on-demand)
- Selfies (no analysis, just flawed self-perception)

## Target Users
**Primary:** Gen Z to Millennials (ages 18-40) who wear makeup regularly and care about their appearance.

**Behavioral Profile:**
- Wear makeup at least 3-4x per week
- Consume beauty content on TikTok/YouTube/Instagram
- Want to improve but lack a feedback loop
- Comfortable paying for self-improvement tools
- Value privacy (not comfortable posting unedited selfies online for feedback)

## Core Value Proposition
**"Your private beauty mirror that sees what you can't."**

Remake gives confident, actionable feedback that makes users feel validated AND gives them a clear path to improvement.

## Onboarding Flow (10+ screens)
1. **Hook** — "You've been watching tutorials. But what does YOUR makeup actually look like?"
2. **Value prop** — "Meet your personal makeup analyst. Private. Honest. Encouraging."
3. **Feature 1** — AI scans your look in seconds
4. **Feature 2** — You get a score + specific suggestions
5. **Feature 3** — Streaks build a habit
6. **Feature 4** — Improvement over time
7. **Social proof** — Testimonials or "X women improved their makeup this month"
8. **Pricing preview** — "Start free. Stay for $4.99/week."
9. **CTA** — "Create your account to start"

## User Journey
1. Complete onboarding (10+ screens)
2. Create account (social login or email)
3. Upload reference photo — Face ID-style setup so AI learns your face
4. Free scan #1
5. See results with soft paywall: "Unlock full breakdown + streak tracking"
6. Pay → unlock detailed category scores + suggestions + start streak

## Detection Categories & Specifics

### Complexion
- Foundation shade match (undertone mismatch, oxidation)
- Coverage evenness (patchy areas, thin spots)
- Primer effectiveness (texture visibility)
- Jawline/hairline/neck blending
- Under-eye concealer creasing/caking
- T-zone shine vs. cheek dryness balance
- Skin finish (dewy/matte harmony)

### Eyes
- Eyeshadow blend seamlessness (harsh lines, patchy areas)
- Eyeshadow shape symmetry (left vs. right)
- Eyeliner wing angle/size symmetry
- Lash line coverage (gaps, uneven mascara)
- Brow symmetry and shape fit
- Under-eye concealer coverage and crease
- Inner corner/tear duct highlight placement

### Lips
- Lip liner precision (bleeding, asymmetry)
- Color application evenness
- Hydration appearance (cracked, dry look)
- Cupid's bow symmetry
- Overlining appropriateness
- Lip color match to overall look

### Sculpt & Glow
- Contour placement and blend (harsh lines, depth appropriateness)
- Blush placement (height, angle for face shape)
- Blush blend uniformity
- Bronzer placement and blend
- Highlighter placement and intensity
- Overall sculpting harmony

**Disclaimer:** Detection accuracy depends on photo quality and lighting. App will warn users to find better lighting if conditions are poor. Flash function available for edge-lit selfies.

## Scoring Psychology
- Scores never go down — always +3 to +8 points above previous scan (randomized within range)
- This combines genuine improvement motivation with psychological safety
- Users feel encouraged, not discouraged

## Retention: Streaks
**Mechanics:**
- Scan with paid scan to maintain streak
- Miss 1 day: streak freezes (can pay to unfreeze once/month)
- Miss 2 days: streak resets

**Milestones:**
| Days | Reward | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| 30 | Streak badge | Cosmetic — new users only, launches in future |
| 60 | $5 off annual coupon | Expires 14 days after trigger |
| 100 | Sephora gift card | Value TBD — tangible real-world reward |

- Coupons stack (e.g., two 100-day milestones = $20 off + $20 gift card)
- Milestone discounts expire 14 days after earning (not 3 days — too punishing for engaged users)

## Flash Function
- Edge-lighting flash like Snapchat for low-light situations
- If lighting is detected as poor, show popup: "Find better lighting for accurate results"
- Flash mode illuminates only edges for a more even, flattering capture

**Aesthetic:** Luxe-girly (soft pinks, warm ivories, gold accents, serif typography)
**Voice:** Like a highly-skilled makeup artist best friend who wants you to succeed
**Tagline:** "Your private beauty mirror"
